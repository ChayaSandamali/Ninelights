Who We Are?

We, at Ninelights, can help take your organization to that next level; making it better, smoother and more efficient through our IT solutions. Ninelights ERP systems and web solutions are used at a number of organizations from a variety of industries with extremely satisfactory results. Our team comprises of highly skilled and motivated individuals who have made it a habit to stay current in today’s rapidly evolving IT field. We offer a comprehensive maintenance package as well.

Thus, we ensure that the latest, cutting-edge technology is used in our solution to your problem; relieving you of the need for constant upgrades while keeping you well ahead of the competition.

Our service team is always willing to help so that you can keep your focus on day-to-day operations at your organization. Leave the IT stuff to us! We understand that every organization is unique; why should you use a system originally designed for someone else? We promise to take into account your specific needs and provide a tailor-made solution for your organization.
